About Us

Christ University Lavasa, located in the scenic hills near Pune, offers a holistic learning environment where academic excellence meets vibrant campus life. Known for its strong emphasis on innovation, leadership, and service, the university prepares students to excel in their chosen fields and make meaningful contributions to society.
